Kasaragod District

Kasaragod District is one of the 14 districts in the state of Kerala, India. Kasaragod became part of Kannur district following the reorganisation of states and formation of Kerala in November 1, 1956. Kasaragod was declared as a district on 24 May 1984.

According to the 2011 census Kasaragod district has a population of 1,307,375,roughly equal to the nation of Mauritius or the US state of New Hampshire.This gives it a ranking of 375th in India (out of a total of 640).
